Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I determine how many portable toilets I need for my event?

A: The number of portable restrooms for events depends on several factors, including the number of attendees, event duration, and whether food and beverages will be served. A general guideline is one portable toilet per 50-75 guests for a typical 4-6 hour event. For longer events or those with alcohol, more units are recommended. We can help you calculate the ideal quantity during your consultation.

Q: What is included in your standard porta potty rental?

A: Our standard porta potty rental units typically include a non-flushable toilet, a urinal, and a hand sanitizer dispenser. They are designed for basic, efficient outdoor sanitation.

Q: Do you offer portable restrooms with sinks for handwashing?

A: Yes, we offer deluxe portable restrooms that include a flushing toilet and a built-in handwashing sink with freshwater, providing a more hygienic and comfortable experience.

Q: Can I rent a portable toilet for just one day?

A: Absolutely! We offer flexible rental periods, including one-day porta potty rental options, perfect for smaller events, construction sites, or short-term needs.

Q: What is the cost of portable toilet rental?

A: The cost of portable toilet rental varies depending on the type of unit, rental duration, quantity needed, and location. We offer competitive porta potty rental prices and can provide a detailed quote upon request.
